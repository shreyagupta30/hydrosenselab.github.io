Faculty and Staff

Manabendra Saharia, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor, Dept. of Civil Engineering

Google Scholar CV Webpage LinkedIn

Dr. Manabendra Saharia is currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. Previously, he worked in the hydrology labs of NASA Goddard Space Flight Center and National Center for Atmospheric Research. Since 2020, he has started setting up the HydroSense Lab at IITD, primarily dedicated to the modeling and mitigation of water-related hazards.
